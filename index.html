Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

Doku (by Listo Labs) is designed to be a private "vault" for your documents.

1. No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or see your personal documents. All files are saved locally on your phone.

2. Local Biometrics: Authentication is handled by iOS/Android system APIs. We never see your Face ID or Fingerprint data.

3. Zero Analytics: We do not track your usage. Your ID locker is your private business.

4. Cloud Backups: If enabled, your data syncs only to your personal Google Drive or iCloud account.
